# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in this project—whether by contributing code, reporting issues, or engaging in discussions.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful.** Treat others with courtesy and professionalism.
- **Be constructive.** Offer feedback that helps improve the project; criticize ideas, not people.
- **Be inclusive.** Welcome newcomers and support diverse perspectives.
- **Collaborate in good faith.** Assume others are acting with good intent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, bullying, or discriminatory language or conduct
- Trolling, personal attacks, or insulting remarks
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing others’ private information without consent
- Other conduct that a reasonable person would find unprofessional or hostile in a community setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action—including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans—when behavior violates this Code of Conduct. All decisions will be made in good faith and are final.

## Scope

This code applies to all project spaces: repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, and any official communication channels.

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have concerns about conduct in this project, please contact the project maintainers. We will treat all reports confidentially and seriously.

---

*This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source practices and may be updated as the project evolves.*
